Log in to your Apple Developer account at https://developer.apple.com/account/.

Click on the "Certificates, IDs & Profiles" section.

Under the "Identifiers" section, select the app identifier for which you want to generate the token.

Scroll down to the "Push Notifications" section and click on the "Configure" button.

Follow the instructions to create a new Push Notification SSL Certificate or use an existing one.

Once you have the certificate, click on the "Generate" button next to the "Token" section.

Download the generated token file (in .p8 format) and keep it secure.

Use the token file in your iOS code to send push notifications to your app
